Ronny's Recipe for Fried Rice

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 2 cups cooked and cooled rice (preferably day-old rice) tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn, and diced bell peppers work well)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preparation:
    • Ensure that your rice is cooked and cooled. Day-old rice works best for fried rice as it tends to be less sticky.
  2. Vegetables:
    • In a large pan or wok, heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil over medium heat.
    • Add the mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until they are tender-crisp. Remove the vegetables from the pan and set aside.
  3. Eggs:
    • In the same pan, add the remaining 1 tablespoon of oil.
    • Pour the beaten eggs into the pan and scramble them. Cook until they are just set.
  4. Combine Ingredients:
    • Add the cooked rice to the pan with the scrambled eggs. Use a spatula to break up any clumps of rice.
  5. Flavoring:
    • Drizzle soy sauce and oyster sauce (if using) over the rice and eggs. Stir well to combine.
    • Add the minced garlic and continue to stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes.
  6. Vegetables Return:
      7. Return the cooked vegetables to the pan and mix them in with the rice and eggs.
  7. Seasoning:
    • Season with salt and pepper to taste. Be cautious with salt as the soy sauce already adds saltiness.
  8. Finish:
    • Drizzle sesame oil over the fried rice and stir to combine.
    • Add chopped green onions and stir once more.
  9. Serve:
    • Once everything is well-mixed and heated through, remove the pan from the heat.
    • Serve the fried rice hot and enjoy!
Feel free to customize the recipe by adding your favorite protein, such as diced chicken, shrimp, or tofu, for an extra boost of flavor and texture.
